


# This is an instructional guide to assemble "Cabana"

  

## Step One

![Image of base](https://github.com/Megameggers/E235/blob/master/IMG_3631.jpeg?raw=true)

* Place the shown pieces as shown​

* Hint: There are 5 pieces

  

## Step Two

  ![step 2](https://github.com/Megameggers/E235/blob/master/IMG_3632.jpeg?raw=true)

* Place one piece on top of the purple piece​

* Then take the other piece and place it on top of both the purple and newly added piece to overlap the green piece

  

## Step Three

![Image of step 2\3](https://github.com/Megameggers/E235/blob/master/IMG_3633.jpeg?raw=true) 
* Add “L” shaped piece with the smallest side attached to the edge of the purple piece​

* Add the green 3X2 behind “L” shape on top of green

  

## Step Four

![Image of step 4](https://github.com/Megameggers/E235/blob/master/IMG_3635.jpeg?raw=true)

* Add tall green piece​

* Add slanted piece

  

## Step Five

![Image of step 5](https://github.com/Megameggers/E235/blob/master/IMG_3635.jpeg?raw=true)

* Add window piece​

* Add orange awning piece

  

## Step Six

![Image of step 6](https://github.com/Megameggers/E235/blob/master/IMG_3637.jpeg?raw=true)

* Add blue rug​

* Add black stepping stone

  

## Congratulations! You’re complete!
